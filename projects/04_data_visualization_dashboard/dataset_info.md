# Supermarket Sales Dataset Information

Historical records of sales in different branches of a supermarket.

## Column Descriptions

- **Branch**: Branch of supercenter (3 branches: A, B and C).
- **City**: Location of supercenters.
- **Customer type**: Type of customers (Member, Normal).
- **Gender**: Gender type of customer.
- **Product line**: General item categorization (Health & Beauty, Electronic accessories, etc.).
- **Unit price**: Price of each unit in $.
- **Quantity**: Number of units purchased.
- **Tax 5%**: Tax fee for customer buying.
- **Total**: Total price including tax.
- **Date**: Date of purchase.
- **Time**: Time of purchase.
- **Payment**: Payment used (Cash, Credit card, Ewallet).
- **gross income**: Gross income from sales.

## Source
Publicly available supermarket sales data.
