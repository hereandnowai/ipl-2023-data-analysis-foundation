## 🎯 Your Mission
Complete the following challenges by writing Python code. Remember: **Data doesn't lie!**

### 🏆 Challenge 1: The Six-Hitting Kings
Who were the most explosive batters of the season?
*   **Task:** Find and display the **Top 10** players with the most number of 6s.
*   **Bonus:** Create a horizontal bar chart (`plt.barh`) to show these power-hitters!

### 💥 Challenge 2: The Most Dramatic Win
Cricket is a game of margins.
*   **Task:** Identify the match with the **highest winning margin by runs** (look at the `winner_runs` column).
*   **Output:** Print the winning team, the opponent, and the margin.

### 🏟️ Challenge 3: The Batter's Paradise
Some stadiums are known for high scores.
*   **Task:** Find the **Average Total Score** for each venue.
*   **Hint:** You'll need to group by `venue` and then calculate the mean of the total runs scored in those matches.
*   **Question:** Which stadium is the "Luckiest" for batters?

### ⚔️ Challenge 4: Battle of the Titans (Virat vs MS Dhoni)
It's time for the ultimate fan-war!
*   **Task:** Compare total runs scored by `Virat Kohli` and `MS Dhoni` in IPL 2023.
*   **Visualization:** Create a simple comparison bar chart.
*   **Note:** Use the `batter` column in the ball-by-ball dataset.

### 🪙 Challenge 5: Does the Toss Matter?
They say "Win the toss, win the match." Is it true?
*   **Task:** Calculate the percentage of matches where the **Toss Winner** also became the **Match Winner**.
*   **Logic:** Count matches where `toss_won == winner`.
