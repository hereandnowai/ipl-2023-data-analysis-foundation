# Time Series Concepts

Time series data is a sequence of observations taken at successive equally spaced points in time.

### Key Concepts
1. **Trend**: The long-term direction of the data (Increasing/Decreasing).
2. **Seasonality**: Repeating patterns (e.g., Ice cream sales increase every summer).
3. **Rolling Averages**: Smoothing out "noise" to see the underlying trend.

### Projects implementation
We'll learn `pd.to_datetime()`, `.set_index()`, and the rolling mean (`.rolling().mean()`).
